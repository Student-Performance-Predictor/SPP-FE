About EduMet

Transforming school management through predictive analytics

EduMet is an advanced student performance prediction system designed specifically for school administrators and educators. Our platform uses machine learning algorithms with 90% accuracy to analyze academic patterns and identify at-risk students before they fall behind.

The system features a comprehensive CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) interface for managing student data, along with role-based authentication for principals, teachers, and administrators.

Our mission is to empower schools with the tools they need to ensure every student reaches their full potential through data-driven decision making.
